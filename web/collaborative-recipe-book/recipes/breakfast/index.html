Breakfast Recipes

Aloo-Paratha

Ingredients: Boiled potatoes, wheat flour, spices (salt, chili, cumin, coriander), oil/ghee.
Knead a soft dough with wheat flour, water, and salt. Mash boiled potatoes and mix with spices and chopped coriander. Roll a dough ball, stuff it with potato filling, and seal. Roll gently into a flat paratha and cook on a hot pan with ghee/oil until golden.

Pav-Bhaji

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, butter, pav buns, spices (pav bhaji masala, chili, turmeric).
Boil and mash vegetables (potatoes, peas, cauliflower). Sauté onions, tomatoes, and spices in butter, then add mashed vegetables. Cook until blended, garnish with butter and coriander. Toast pav buns with butter and serve with bhaji, lemon, and onions.

Puri-Chola

Ingredients for Puri: Wheat flour, water, oil.
For Chola: Boiled chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, spices (cumin, garam masala). Knead dough for puris, roll into small discs, and deep-fry until puffed. For chola, sauté onions and spices, add tomatoes, and simmer. Mix in boiled chickpeas and cook for 10-15 minutes.

Idli-Sambhar

Ingredients for Idli: Rice and urad dal batter.
For Sambar: Lentils (toor dal), tamarind, vegetables, sambar masala. Steam idli batter in molds for 10-12 minutes. For sambar, cook toor dal, add tamarind pulp, spices, and cooked vegetables. Temper with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and red chilies. Serve hot.

Sattu-Paratha

Ingredients: Sattu (roasted gram flour), wheat flour, spices (cumin, chili, ajwain)
Knead a soft dough with wheat flour and water. Mix sattu with spices, lemon juice, and a little water to make a stuffing. Roll a dough ball, stuff it with sattu mixture, and seal. Roll into a flat paratha and cook on a hot pan with ghee/oil
