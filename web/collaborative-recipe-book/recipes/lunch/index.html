Lunch Recipes

Vegetable Pulao & Raita

Ingredients for Pulao: Basmati rice, mixed vegetables, whole spices, ghee.
Ingredients for Raita: Yogurt, cucumber, salt, cumin powder.
Pulao: Sauté spices and vegetables in ghee, add rice and water, and cook until done. Raita: Mix grated cucumber, yogurt, and spices.

Dahi & Phulka

Ingredients for Curd : Curd, red chilli powder, chat masala, garam masala, cumin.
Ingredients for Phulka: Wheat flour, water, salt.
Curd : Mix curd with spices and masalas. Phulka: Roll dough into circles and cook on a skillet.

Kadhi Pakora & Jeera Rice

Ingredients for Kadhi: Yogurt, gram flour, turmeric, curry leaves.
Ingredients for Pakora: Gram flour, onions, spices.
Kadhi: Cook yogurt and gram flour with spices, temper with curry leaves. Pakora: Deep-fry spiced gram flour batter. Add to kadhi.

Egg Curry & Rice

Ingredients for Egg Curry: Boiled eggs, onions, tomatoes, spices (turmeric, garam masala).
Ingredients for Rice: Basmati rice, water, salt.
Egg Curry: Sauté onions and tomatoes with spices, then add boiled eggs and simmer. Rice: Boil rice with water and salt until fluffy.

Dal Khichdi & Pickle

Ingredients for Khichdi: Rice, moong dal, turmeric, ghee, cumin.
Ingredients for Pickle: Store-bought or homemade pickle.
Khichdi: Cook rice and dal with spices, temper with ghee and cumin. Pickle: Serve alongside for added flavor.
