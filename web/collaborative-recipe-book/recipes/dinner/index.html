Dinner Recipes

Bhindi Masala & Phulka

Ingredients for Bhindi Masala: Okra, onions, tomatoes, spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander).
Ingredients for Phulka: Wheat flour, water.
Bhindi Masala: Sauté chopped okra until dry, then cook with spiced onion-tomato masala. Phulka: Roll dough into thin circles and cook on a skillet until puffed

Rajma & Jeera Rice

Ingredients for Rajma: Kidney beans, onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, spices.
Ingredients for Jeera Rice: Basmati rice, cumin seeds, ghee.
Rajma: Cook kidney beans and simmer in spiced onion-tomato gravy for 20 minutes. Jeera Rice: Cook rice and toss with cumin seeds sautéed in ghee.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients: Chicken, basmati rice, yogurt, fried onions, whole spices, saffron.
Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices. Cook chicken and layer with half-cooked rice. Add fried onions and saffron milk, then steam for 20 minutes.

Egg Curry & Rice

Ingredients for Egg Curry: Boiled eggs, onions, tomatoes, spices (turmeric, garam masala).
Ingredients for Rice: Basmati rice, water, salt.
Egg Curry: Sauté onions and tomatoes with spices, then add boiled eggs and simmer. Rice: Boil rice with water and salt until fluffy.

Paneer Butter Masala & Naan

Ingredients for Paneer: Paneer, tomato, cream, butter, spices.
Ingredients for Naan: Flour, yeast, yogurt, water.
Paneer: Blend sautéed tomato and spices into a paste, cook with cream and paneer. Naan: Knead dough, let it rise, roll, and cook on a skillet or tandoor.
